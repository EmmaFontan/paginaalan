How To Buy

Create and top up your wallet

Choose a Wallet, it can be Trust wallet or MetaMask

Download it from AppStore or Google Play Store for Mobile devices. Pc users, use google extension instead.

Get some BNB

If you don’t have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet , or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Pancakeswap

Connect to Pancakeswap.

Go to pancakeswap.finance in google chrome or in the browser inside your Metamask app..

Connect your wallet.

Paste the $MYRO2.0 tokenaddress into Pancakeswap, select Myro, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap BNB for MYRO

Swap your BNB for $MYRO2.0. although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
